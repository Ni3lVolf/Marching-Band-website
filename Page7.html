Whistle Commands (for Drum Majors)

We will use whistle commands for two purposes

When battery is playing a street cadence a long whistle will tell them to finish set street cadence and go back to Marching Cadence

During Marching Cadence a long whistle tells the ensemble to get their instruments upa dn get ready to play whatever piece has been aligned for them to play during the parade

Voice commands

These commands are to only be used at set or the position of attention
