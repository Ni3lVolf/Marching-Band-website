Welcome to the season! Please review our offical polocies, expectations, and requirments below

1. Leadership Expectations:

  • Central DrumMajor Expectations: I want the band to be able to come together and work with one another in a way that shows morale and builds confidence amongst one another.

  • Rear DrumMajor Expectations: One of my expectations is for everyone is to become better listeners. Because it shows in our performances who listens and who doesn’t.

  • Flute Section Leader Expectations:DNF

  • Clarinet Section Leader Expectations:My expectations for the band this year are to work hard and enjoy their time being a part of the band.

  • Saxophone Section Leader Expectations:My expectations for the band is for everyone to open minded adn respectful

  • High Brass Section Leader Expectations:I expect everyone to have discipline and alot more accountability

  • Low Brass Section Leader Expectations:Everyone should be able to give and take constructive criticism throught the band to identify our weakpoints

  • DrumLine captain Expectations:I want everyone to become more skillful not only rhythmically but also musically

  • Historian Expectations:I want everyone to have a fun time and be proud of being in this band! We need to work our hardest to give them the skills to be great and be proud

  • Equipment Manager Expectations:One of My expectations is for everyone to have consistancy and work hard.

    • 2. Uniform & Dress Code:

    When wearing the Yellow Jacket regiment unifor, you represent our entire school and community. Meaning whilst in unifrom you are a reflection of School and this Program

  • Rehearsal: Athletic shoes (NO sandals/boots), breathable clothing, and your DotBook/music.
  • Performance: Shako, Long black socks, clean marching shoes (dinkles), No visible jewlery or long hair down.
